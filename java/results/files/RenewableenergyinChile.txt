renewable energy in chile is a fast growing sector that in 2014 provided 9% of the countrys electricity chile has vast geothermal solar and wind energy resources while fossil fuel resources are limited it has a legislated renewable energy target of 20% by 2025 with 15% of the worlds volcanoes chile has vast geothermal potential estimated at 16,000 mw this includes the el tatio geyser field with 8% of the worlds geysers in 2014 a 100 mw photovoltaic plant located near copiap in the atacama desert was inaugurated it was the largest in latin america at the time the 115 mw el arrayn wind farm is the largest in chile and at the time of its inauguration in 2014 it was the largest in latin america media related to renewable energy in chile at wikimedia commons
